Privacy Policy for MediaSync Pro

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which MediaSync Pro operates as an application ("App"). MediaSync Pro is designed to facilitate the installation of media files from online sources. It does not collect, use, maintain, or disclose any personal information from its users (each, a "User") in any form.

Information Collection and Use:

MediaSync Pro does not collect any personal identification information from Users. It operates solely to assist Users in installing media files from online sources to their specified folders.

User Data and Information:

MediaSync Pro does not store, modify, or access any media files or links provided by Users. It merely facilitates the installation process based on the links provided by the User.

Internet and Permissions:

MediaSync Pro requires internet access and full trust permissions to function effectively. These permissions are solely used for the purpose of accessing online media files and installing them to the user-specified folders. MediaSync Pro does not utilize these permissions for any other purposes.
